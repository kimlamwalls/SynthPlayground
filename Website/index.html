    What is SynthLab?

    Who are we?

    SynthLab are a collective not for profit group of musicians and music enthusiasts based in Melbourne, Australia. Who aspires to share knowledge with like-minded individuals.

    What do we do?

    We run a range of classes and programs that teach people how to use synthesizers and other musical equipment in order for them to unleash their musical creativity!
    We also offer free online educational resources and tools for people to get a taste of working with electronic synthesizers
